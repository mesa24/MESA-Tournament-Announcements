# MPS: Opening 2026 - Official Tournament Announcement

**Tournament Operator:** MESA  
**Announcement Date:** January 12, 2026

---

## Tournament Dates

**Open Qualifier:** January 27-29, 2026 (Online)  
**Main Event:** February 3-6, 2026 (LAN)

---

## Additional Information

### Direct VRS Invites
**None.** This tournament does not feature any Direct VRS Invites or Wildcard Invites.

### VRS Information for Seeding
- **Seeding VRS Publication Date:** January 5, 2026
- VRS-ranked rosters will be seeded higher than non-VRS rosters in the Open Qualifier
- Among VRS-ranked teams, seeding will follow their Global VRS ranking

### Visa Region
Mongolia

### Tournament Stages
- **Open Qualifier:** Online
- **Main Event:** LAN

### Prize Pool
**Total Prize Pool:** $3,000 USD

**Distribution:**
- 1st Place: $1,650
- 2nd Place: $750
- 3rd Place: $375
- 4th Place: $225

**Additional Compensation:** None. No appearance fees, revenue shares, or other forms of compensation beyond the prize pool.

### Open Qualifier Rules
- **Registration:** mesa.mn
- **Registration Fee:** 50,000 MNT (Mongolian bank transfer)
- **Registration Period:** January 18-25, 2026
- **No team limit**
- **Advancement:** Top 8 teams qualify for Main Event

**Format:** Details to be announced with registration

### Tournament Format

**Group Stage:**
- Two double-elimination (GSL) groups
- 4 teams per group
- Opening matches: Bo1
- Following matches: Bo3
- Top 2 teams per group advance to Playoffs

**Playoffs:**
- Double-Elimination bracket
- All matches: Bo3

### Integrity and Conduct Rules
All participants must comply with the **MESA CS General Rulebook** (Sections 56-71).

**Disqualification criteria include:**
- Cheating or prohibited software use
- Match fixing
- Doping violations
- Betting on tournament matches
- Competition manipulation
- Code of Conduct violations

These rules are transparent, objective, and applied equally to all participants. Full details available in the MESA CS General Rulebook..
